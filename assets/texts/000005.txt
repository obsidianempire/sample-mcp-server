INVESTMENT ADVISORY AGREEMENT
Agreement Number: INV-2022



PARTIES:
Jane Smith ("Client") and Alpha Advisory LLC ("Advisor") effective March 1, 2022.


SECTION 1: SERVICES
Advisor will provide investment strategy, portfolio management, and risk assessment.
Advisor shall act in accordance with industry best practices.


SECTION 2: FIDUCIARY DUTY
Advisor agrees to act in the best interest of the client, maintaining fiduciary obligations.
Advisor shall disclose any conflicts of interest.


SECTION 3: FEES
Annual advisory fee: 1% of assets under management.
Performance-based fee: 10% of gains exceeding benchmark.


SECTION 4: TERMINATION
Agreement requires a 60-day written notice for termination without penalty.
Termination for cause may occur immediately upon breach.


SECTION 5: CONFIDENTIALITY
Advisor shall maintain confidentiality of all client information.


SECTION 6: GOVERNING LAW
Agreement governed by laws of the State of Delaware.
